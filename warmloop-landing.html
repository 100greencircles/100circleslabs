How it works

The monthly loop

WarmLoop runs a full relationship cycle every month — automatically drafting, sending, and capturing conversations across your circle.

1

Import & map your contacts

Connect LinkedIn, WhatsApp, email. WarmLoop reads who's there and which channel you use with each person.

2

Tag each person's tier

From "know them" to "2am friend" — or let the AI suggest based on interaction history.

3

Week 1: Loop opens

Personalised conversation starters go out on the right channel — sharing your update, asking about theirs.

4

Week 2: Loop closes

Replies are captured, context stored. Intros, collabs, and follow-ups are surfaced automatically.

5

Repeat — warmer each time

Every cycle, WarmLoop knows more. Messages get sharper. Relationships get deeper.
